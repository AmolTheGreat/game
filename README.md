A really fun natural disaster game where random disasters spawn about every 30 seconds. The goal is to survive. 
To go forward and backward use the arrow keys, to jump press the space bar, to chop the trees click on the brown squares, and finally to go in the shop and exit the shop press k.
Buy shelters and powerups using wood to make it easier to survive longer and get a high score!
Updates that are comming will be challenge mode and some disasters buffed!
